About the Event
TechCon 2025 is a premier technology and programming conference bringing together developers, innovators, students, and industry leaders for a dynamic exchange of ideas, learning, and networking. Organized by GeeksforGeeks, this annual event is designed to celebrate technology, inspire innovation, and empower the next generation of tech professionals.
